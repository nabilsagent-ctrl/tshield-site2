How It Began

Topaz Detailing started 15 years ago with a single mission: create the world's highest-standard protective films. What began as a boutique service for luxury automotive owners has evolved into a global standard.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the total investment required?
Initial franchise investment ranges from £50,000 to £150,000, depending on your territory and business model. This covers training, initial inventory, equipment, licensing, and working capital. We'll provide a detailed breakdown during your consultation.
How long is the training program?
Initial training is 2 weeks onsite at our headquarters. This covers technical application, business operations, sales, and marketing. After launch, you'll participate in quarterly workshops and have unlimited access to our technical support team.
What's the franchise term and renewal?
Initial term is 10 years with renewal options. We review performance annually and provide support to ensure you hit profitability targets. Renewal terms are negotiated based on your track record and our mutual interests.
Do I need prior experience with protective film?
No. We train you from the ground up. What we need is your commitment to excellence, sales ability, and willingness to learn. Many of our most successful franchisees came from entirely different backgrounds.
What territories are available?
We're currently offering franchises in the UK with plans to expand internationally. Territory allocation is based on population density and market potential. Contact us to discuss specific regions.
How profitable can a T-Shield franchise be?
Profitability depends on your market, effort, and sales execution. Mature franchisees typically achieve EBITDA margins of 35–45% on installation revenue, with maintenance plans providing recurring, high-margin revenue. We'll share detailed projections during your consultation.
What ongoing royalties do I pay?
Royalties are 6% of gross revenue, capped at £2,000 monthly. This covers ongoing support, training, technology, and marketing resources. You'll also purchase inventory from us at wholesale rates, creating margin on retail sales.
