Innovation

A New Category in Surface Protection

For decades, protective film has been revolutionising exterior automotive surfaces. T-Shield brings this proven technology to interior surfaces—creating an entirely new category in the protective film industry.

Just as PPF protects car paint from damage, T-Shield protects interior surfaces from scratches, stains, heat damage, and daily wear. It's invisible, durable, and removes cleanly without damage.

From kitchen countertops to hotel suite walls, from luxury car interiors to commercial office desks—the surfaces that matter most in people's lives now have world-class protection.

Origin

How It All Began

The concept for T-Shield emerged from conversations with Topaz customers who had invested in premium finishes, bespoke kitchens, and luxury interiors. They had one recurring frustration: protecting their investments.

"We use PPF on our cars, but what about our home?" they asked.

That question sparked something. Our research team spent two years developing T-Shield—a transparent protective film engineered for interior surfaces, removing cleanly without damage, and delivering the same level of protection that made PPF the industry standard for automobiles.

The market response has been overwhelming. Architects specify it. Luxury homeowners request it. Hospitality operators are adopting it at scale. Today, we're inviting the right franchise partners to help us meet the demand.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior installation experience to become a T-Shield partner?

+

No, but you need aptitude and commitment to learning. We provide comprehensive hands-on training covering all technical skills. Previous experience in trades, automotive PPF, or related fields is advantageous but not required. What matters is your dedication to mastering the craft and delivering excellent customer experiences.

What's the typical project timeline and customer acquisition cycle?

+

Most installations take 1–5 days depending on scope. Customer acquisition timelines vary: residential projects may move 4–8 weeks from lead to install; hospitality and commercial projects 8–16 weeks due to decision-making layers. Our marketing support accelerates lead generation from day one.

How do I access and maintain my inventory of T-Shield film?

+

You'll order inventory directly from our distribution network at franchise partner rates. We recommend starting with a curated initial stock covering the most common applications and surface types. As you grow and understand your market, you'll adjust your inventory mix. Volume discounts increase as you scale.

What does a typical customer's lifetime value look like?

+

Highly variable, but consider: initial installation (£3,500–£25,000+ depending on project), maintenance contracts (£500–£2,000 annually), upsells (additional surface expansion), and referrals. A single residential client can generate £12,000–£40,000 in lifetime revenue. Commercial/hospitality clients generate more. Building recurring service revenue is critical to franchise profitability.

How competitive is the market for interior surface protection right now?

+

Very low. T-Shield is the category leader with minimal direct competition. Your competitive advantage comes from being first, carrying the trusted Topaz brand, and having access to our support network. This is the ideal time to establish market dominance in your territory before other players emerge.

What training and ongoing support does Topaz provide?

+

Initial certification covers installation techniques, surface assessment, customer consultation, warranty protocols, and safety standards. Ongoing support includes monthly check-ins, quarterly business reviews, technical support, access to our partner network, continuous training for product innovations, and marketing collaboration. We're invested in your success.

Are there geographical territory protections?

+

Yes. We define initial territory based on your business plan, local market potential, and our growth strategy. Territory protections ensure you have room to build your business without internal cannibalization. As your territory matures, expansion opportunities and sub-franchising may be available.
